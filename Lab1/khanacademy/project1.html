Facts about USA

  1. The USA is the third largest country in the world, covering an area of 9.8 million square kilometers.
  2. It’s also the world’s largest economy, accounting for about 24% of the world’s GDP.
  3. The capital of the USA is Washington, D.C., while the largest city is New York City.
  4. The USA is a federal republic, consisting of 50 states and one federal district.
  5. The USA is home to some of the world’s most prestigious universities, including Harvard, Yale, and Stanford.
